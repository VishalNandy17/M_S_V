Crisis Support in India

If you're experiencing a mental health crisis or need immediate support, please use the resources below. Remember, you're not alone, and help is available.

Emergency Services

If you're in immediate danger, call 112 for emergency services in India.

Crisis Helplines

  • AASRA: 91-9820466726 (24/7 Helpline)
  • Vandrevala Foundation: 1860-2662-345 or 1800-2333-330
  • iCall: +91 22 2556 3291 (Monday to Saturday, 10 AM to 8 PM)
  • Sneha Foundation: 044-24640050 (24/7 Helpline)

Online Resources

  • AASRA - Mental Health Support
  • Vandrevala Foundation
  • The Live Love Laugh Foundation

Immediate Coping Strategies

  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Use grounding techniques (e.g., 5-4-3-2-1 method)
  • Reach out to a trusted friend or family member
  • Write down your thoughts and feelings
  • Try meditation or yoga techniques

Connect with a Professional

If you need to speak with a mental health professional, we can help you find one in your area.
